Tournament
----------
- id: [unique identifier]
- name: [string]
- date: [date]
- type: [string, e.g., 'Singles', 'Doubles']
- totalPlayers: [integer, total number of players who entered the tournament]
- matches: [array of match IDs]


Match
-----
- id: [unique identifier]
- matchType: [string, e.g., 'Singles', 'Doubles']
- playersTeamA: [array of one or two player IDs for Team A]
- playersTeamB: [array of one or two player IDs for Team B]
- scoresTeamA: [integer, total score for Team A]
- scoresTeamB: [integer, total score for Team B]
- status: [string, e.g., 'pending', 'ongoing', 'completed']
- timeouts: [array of timeouts with player IDs and timestamps]
- wipes: [array of wipes with player IDs and timestamps]

Player
------
- id: [unique identifier]
- name: [string]
- email: [string, optional]
- categoryRank: [string, e.g., 'A', 'B', 'C']
- overallRank: [integer, player's ranking within the category]
